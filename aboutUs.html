About us

Andreas (Passkas) and Anna Panteli, owners of Romios Holiday house, Apartments welcome you to their holiday villa in one of the most beautiful villages of Cyprus, Kalavasos! Our luxury self contained holiday apartments are situated just a few minutes walk from the village square where you can find mainy traditional kafenions (coffee shops), a mini supermarket that sells everything you need many traditional restaurants and taverns, a wellness centre, and much more.


About Kalavasos

Kalavasos is a picturesque village ideally situated for your holiday, right in the heart of Cyprus.

  • Just thirty minutes drive from the island's main airport Larnaca. Locally you can enjoy, various activities, including fishing, hiking, nature trails, horse riding, golf, cycling, olive and fruit picking in our orchard, See how haloumi cheese is made.
  • The local Governers beach is only five minutes drive and ten minutes drive you will find Zigi fishing village, visit kalavasos's Archeological tenta, and have a scenic relaxing picnic at the damn.
  • Two minutes drive to the motorway which opens your journeys to explore the beautiful places and adventures Cyprus has to offer you.
